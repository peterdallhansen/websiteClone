Zonify.ai has launched two new analytics tools designed to provide deeper insights into visitor behavior:

*   **Cross Visitor Flow**: Provides insights into end-to-end visitor flows across multiple shops or areas, tracking how patterns and synergies evolve over time.
*   **Visitor Dwell Time**: Measures visitors’ shopping time in specific shops or zones to reveal typical and peak stay durations.

These metrics serve as key KPIs for operators. The integration of this data ensures that the Zonify.ai suite of features and insights remains comprehensive and market-compliant.
