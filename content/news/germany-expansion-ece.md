Germany represents an important first step into Central European markets for Zonify.ai.

The team recently visited Hamburg, including ECE’s headquarters, to present the KYC Performance Management platform, which is designed to drive earnings while reducing operational risk.

The meeting provided an opportunity to engage with key stakeholders, including Jan Hambach, and to gain deeper insights into ECE and the broader market.

In 2025, Zonify.ai plans to expand its network within the German shopping center, retail, and airport sectors.
