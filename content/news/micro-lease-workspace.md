Zonify.ai has introduced a new Micro Lease workspace, designed to help retailers, malls, and airports maximize the commercial value of their in-store media and pop-up shop spaces.

Key highlights include:

*   Increasing leasing income by offering credible, data-backed visitor and audience insights.
*   Measuring impressions, dwell time, and engagement at key media and leasing zones.
*   Analyzing visitor flows, audience profiles, and demographic reach across surrounding areas.
*   Optimizing campaign performance and pricing using transparent, week-by-week visitor data.
*   Identifying, validating, and documenting new relevant leasing zones or media placements using historical data.

The workspace aims to assist operators and landlords in converting underused spaces into high-performing media assets and direct revenue streams, without requiring additional hardware or operational complexity.
