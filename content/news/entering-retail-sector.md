Zonify.ai has entered the retail sector through a collaboration with a leading Danish retailer.

Parallel to its focus on shopping malls, the company is now addressing the challenge of transforming business intelligence within physical retail using AI. This initiative aims to help clients stay ahead of competitors and remain relevant to their customer base.
