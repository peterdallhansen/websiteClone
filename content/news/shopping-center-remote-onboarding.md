title: "First Fully Remote Shopping Center Onboarding Completed"
date: "2025-10-22"
company: "Zonify.ai"
image: "/images/articles/remote-onboarding.png"
---

Zonify.ai has successfully completed the first-ever fully remote onboarding of a shopping center, covering more than 40,000 m² and integrating over 50 video cameras without a single on-site visit.

Leveraging AI-powered footfall software and a scalable software-only architecture, this deployment demonstrates the ability to reduce costs, simplify deployment, and accelerate scaling for shopping center operators and retail asset managers.

Processes that previously required weeks or months, often involving hardware installations and complex setup, were completed in just a few days. This achievement marks a significant step toward democratizing access to high-quality footfall data across retail spaces.

Remote onboarding represents a key element in the mission to transform the industry, setting a new benchmark for affordability, scalability, and innovation.
